About Us

At NAMING, we carry out a multidisciplinary work composed of highly skilled specialists in various areas. We work collaboratively and passionately to provide the best development and advice to brands from all sectors.

A team of professionals and consultants committed to the legacy of Diego Armando Maradona and his brand identity.

Identity

Sustainable: Our work system self-funds through the advisory operations we provide to the investments of business units.

Mission: To create an environment for analysis, supervision, support, and reception of the business units that make up the Maradona brand and others.

Vision: We seek to promote actions that foster transparency and profitability of existing business units, as well as conduct feasibility analysis of new projects and offer support to investors of the Maradona brand, in line with the strategic objectives of Sattvica S.A.

Our Services

Analysis and Viability Assessment

We conduct a comprehensive market analysis and evaluate the feasibility of the business idea, identifying opportunities and challenges to establish a solid foundation.

Strategic Planning

We develop detailed business plans, setting clear objectives, effective marketing strategies, and concrete actions to achieve business success.

Business Model Design

We help define the appropriate business model, considering customer segments, value proposition, revenue sources, and cost structure to maximize profitability.

Marketing Strategy Development

We create personalized marketing strategies to position the brand, identify target audiences, generate demand, and establish a competitive advantage in the market.

Financial Advisory

We provide guidance in financial management, including cash flow projections, profitability analysis, financing strategies, and the search for potential investors.

Project Implementation and Management

We support the implementation of defined strategies and actions, providing a structured and effective approach to manage projects and achieve established goals.

Monitoring and Continuous Improvement

We track key performance indicators and provide recommendations for ongoing business improvement, identifying growth opportunities and optimization.

Enhancing Customer Experience

The consultancy works on identifying and understanding customer needs, designing strategies to improve their experience. This includes developing loyalty programs, implementing Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems, and enhancing customer service processes.
